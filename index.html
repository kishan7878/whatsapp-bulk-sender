Frequently Asked Questions

Is this tool free?

Yes! It's completely free and open-source. You can use, modify, and distribute it freely.

Will my account get banned?

Use responsibly with proper delays (3-5 seconds). Don't send spam or exceed 100-200 messages per day.

What file types can I send?

You can send APKs, PDFs, images, videos, documents - any file type WhatsApp supports.

How many contacts can I add?

There's no limit! However, we recommend batches of 100-200 per day to avoid rate limiting.

Is my data secure?

Yes! Everything runs locally on your machine. No data is sent to external servers.

Can I customize messages?

Yes! You can use templates with variables and personalize each message.
