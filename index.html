Frequently Asked Questions

Is this tool free?

Yes! It's completely free and open-source. You can use, modify, and distribute it freely.

Is it safe to use?

Yes! Everything runs locally on your computer. No data is sent to external servers. Your WhatsApp session is secure.

Will my account get banned?

Use responsibly with proper delays (5-10 seconds). Don't send spam or exceed 100-200 messages per day.

What file types can I send?

You can send APKs, PDFs, images, videos, documents - any file type WhatsApp supports.

How many contacts can I add?

There's no limit! However, we recommend batches of 100-200 per day to avoid rate limiting.

Does it work on Mac/Linux?

Currently Windows only. Mac and Linux versions are planned for future releases.
